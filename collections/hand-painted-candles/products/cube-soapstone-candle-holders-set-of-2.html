    Cube Soapstone Candle Holders, Set of 2

    $ 26.99

    Hand-carved by African artisans from a solid piece of natural Soapstone, this set of two square-shaped candle holders are a work of art!

    The soapstone rock is ideal for carving as it gets its softness from the mineral talc and is commonly believed to have a soothing, balancing effect while creating a positive, calming energy.

    Place several together to create a truly unique statement.

    Each candle holder measures approximately 2.5-inches by 2.5-inches. 

    • As a natural material, the color of soapstone varies from creamy white, to rosy pink, to earthy gold.
    • Due to the handmade nature of this item, each is unique and will vary slightly.
    • Each cube holds 1 taper candle.
    • Fair Trade.
    • Use caution: Surface may become warm or hot during use; place on a protected surface. Keep away from children and pets. Never leave a burning candle unattended.
